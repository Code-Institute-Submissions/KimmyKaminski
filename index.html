Kimmy Kaminski


Music Tutor Aberdeen & Aberdeenshire

Kimmy has a wide range of experience when it comes to musical styles, including jazz, punk, heavy metal, classical and funk rock. She has played in bands for almost 10 years and has performed at the O2 arena in London and supported pop group "The Saturdays". She has completed a Higher National Diploma in Music and is currently finishing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Music. Her other music qualifications include Rock School Grades, ABRSM Grade 5 Music Theory and Avid Training certificate in Pro Tools 10.
