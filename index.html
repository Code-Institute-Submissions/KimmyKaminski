Kimmy Kaminski


Music Tutor Session Musician

Kimmy has a range of experience when it comes to musical styles such as jazz, punk, heavy metal, classical and funk rock. She has played in various bands for almost 10 years performing at the O2 arena in London and supporting pop group "The Saturdays". She has completed a Higher National Diploma in Music and is currently finishing a Bachelor of Arts degree in Music. Her other music qualifications include Rock School Grades, ABRSM Grade 5 Music Theory and an Avid Training certificate in Pro Tools 10.
