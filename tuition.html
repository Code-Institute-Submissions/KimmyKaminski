Tuition

Bass Lessons

Kimmy has over 10 years’ experience playing the bass and offers one to one lessons. She can adjust teaching styles to accommodate your own specific goals, whether that be to complete grades and learn how to read music or simply to learn how to play your favourite songs. Kimmy's is qualified to teach up to grade 8. Get in touch on the contact page to arrange a lesson.
Face to face or virtual lessons available: £15 per 30 mins.

Guitar Lessons

Kimmy offers one to one lessons for the guitar and can teach up to grade 5. Whether you want to achieve grades on the guitar, covering basics of theory, or you want to learn specific songs, Kimmy will provide a friendly and relaxed atmosphere to do so. Get in touch on the contact page to arrange a lesson.
Face to face or virtual lessons available: £15 per 30 mins.

Piano Lessons

Kimmy teaches Piano up to grade 3 and will tailor lessons to meet your aspirations. Lessons can be focussed on your preferred style of music and progress at a pace which is comfortable for each student. Get in touch on the contact page to arrange a lesson.
Face to face or virtual lessons available: £15 per 30 mins.

Music Theory Lessons

Kimmy has completed ABRSM grade 5 Music Theory exam, and is currently working towards grade 8. She is able to confidently teach music theory up to grade 5. Kimmy can teach theory either alongside instrument lessons, or on its own. Get in touch on the contact page to arrange a lesson.
Face to face or virtual lessons available: £15 per 30 mins.
